I'm the founding product designer at OverseeAI, where I design an enterprise agentic platform for insurance teams — AI agents run the work; human experts stay in control. I own design end to end: the product, the design system, and the language.

My focus is what happens after AI does the work — how an expert verifies it, trusts it, and signs off on it. And I build design systems machines can read: the Figma library, DESIGN.md, and token files are the spec, and AI coding agents read them on every build.

Before OverseeAI I shipped consumer products at scale. My redesigns cut customer service calls 37% at ICICI Lombard and 70% at Axis Bank, and lifted Lenskart's home-to-listing conversion 14%. OverseeAI hired that track record to build its design function from zero.
